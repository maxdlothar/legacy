Inform Excellence Drive

Welcome to the launch of Inform's Excellence Drive, our next step in galvanising Inform to meet the challenges of the future.

The purpose of this initiative is to provide all our team with the right support, training and tools to assist in the successful growth of our business going forward. We are pleased to welcome on board Jo Haythornthwaite who will be helping to make these things happen.

Jo is both highly qualified and experienced in working in the market place and her skills and abilities, I hope, will be much appreciated and will help us all to achieve the key objectives and targets we want to realise from this.

We see, in going forward, you, our team members, as the most valued asset in the company to make this happen and enable the ongoing security and expansion of the business. A strong business will provide all of us with the peace of mind and security for the future we all want and need.

Some of the areas we wish to work on together are:

  • Individual training plans for all of you.
  • Training to assist you in your daily work.
  • Regular reviews to build your competencies and abilities, enabling you to grow your skills and talents.
  • Regular team meetings to keep you informed of how we are doing and giving you the opportunity to feedback to us the things that can improve our business and take us forward.
  • Complete review of all procedures and processes to document and make available electronically.
  • Improved communications, both verbal and written.

Jo’s first day here was on Monday, 5th March 2007 and she will be in the office two days a week. Listed below are dates for the next month. She will commence one-to-ones with all of you to give you the opportunity to provide constructive comments that will assist this exciting initiative which I am sure we all want to be successful. Your role in this is critical and I do encourage all of you to be as committed as you can so we can really make a difference and improve our business.
